Betrayal at a Mansion on a Mountain

Betrayal at a Mansion on a Mountain is an adventure/sci-fi/horror board game.
Players start the game as allies while they explore the mansion and look for clues and tools to help them later in their adventure.
Once a special event has been triggered, that is when the betrayal really begins!
One player then becomes the "betrayer" and the game becomes a one versus all. The goal of the "betrayer" is generally to kill all the other players.
But, if the players can manage to foil the plans of the "betrayer", the players win and the day is saved!
