Fascists vs. Liberals

Fascists vs. Liberals is a social deduction party game. Generally, the gameplay is more fun if you can get the maximum number of possible players.
To start, all players are randomly, and secretly, given an ID card telling them if they are a Liberal or a Fascist for that game.
There are tiles that have Liberal or Fascist written on them. These represent policies that need to be passed.
Two players participate in each round and then play proceeds clockwise.
The first of the two players picks up the top three tiles from the policy deck and discards one and then hands the other two to the second player.
The second player then has to choose from the remaining two tiles which policy to pass. Liberals want to pass Liberal policies, and Fascists want to pass Fascist policies.
Whichever of the two sides passes five of their policies first, wins!
