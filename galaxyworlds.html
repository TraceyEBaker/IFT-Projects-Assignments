Galaxy Worlds

Galaxy Worlds in one of my favorite two player games! It's a deck building game where you act as a defender of space!
There are several different factions of spaceships under your control. Some factions are powerful fighters, while other factions' strength lies in their defense.
You and your opponent will take alternating turns to attempt to do damage to each other. You will start with 50 health, and whomever runs out of health first, loses.
There is also a free mobile version of this game, but extra expansions and online play will be charged a small fee.
